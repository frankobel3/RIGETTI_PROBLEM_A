\section*{Problem}

Compute the Maximum Power Energy Section (MPES) of a 21-node grid modeled as a weighted graph.

\[
\text{Cut}(S) = \sum_{(i,j)\in E} w_{ij}\,\mathbf{1}[i \in S, j \notin S]
\]

\[
\text{MPES} = \frac{\text{Cut}(S^*)}{\sum w_{ij}}
\]

\section*{Pipeline}

\begin{itemize}
\item Exact optimal cut via half-enumeration
\item Plateau verification ($k \le 4$ flips)
\item Trust metrics: balance, redundancy, N-1 resilience, bottleneck
\item Optional QAOA bridge
\end{itemize}
